# Final Project – Neuroscience as a Model for AI

## Problem
The purpose of this final project was to bring together all concepts learned throughout the course and apply neuroscience principles to artificial intelligence.

## Approach
I applied concepts such as memory, attention, perception, and cognitive processing to analyze how AI systems function compared to the human brain.

## Results
The project demonstrated how AI systems can simulate certain human cognitive functions but still differ in how information is processed and understood.

## What I Learned
From this final project, I learned how neuroscience concepts directly influence AI design and development. I also gained a deeper understanding of the strengths and limitations of artificial intelligence systems.
