# L02 – AI Lab Work

## Problem
This lab focused on understanding basic artificial intelligence concepts and how AI systems process information.

## Approach
I followed the lab instructions and worked through the exercises to observe how AI responds to different types of input. I tested how the system interprets and generates responses.

## Results
The results showed that AI systems process input based on patterns and learned data. The system was able to generate responses that followed the structure of the input.

## What I Learned
From this lab, I learned how AI systems generate responses and how they rely on patterns rather than true understanding. This helped me better understand how AI works behind the scenes.
