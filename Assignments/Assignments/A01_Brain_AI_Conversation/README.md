# A01 – Brain vs AI Conversation

## Problem
This assignment focused on comparing how the human brain and AI systems learn, remember, and make decisions.

## Approach
I created a dialogue between the brain and an AI system to explain differences in learning, memory, and decision-making.

## Results
The conversation showed that the brain learns continuously through experience, while AI depends on training data and patterns.

## What I Learned
I learned that AI systems are fast and useful, but the human brain is more flexible and adaptive.
