My name is Brandy Griffin, and I am a student studying Artificial Intelligence. This portfolio highlights my work throughout the course, where I explored how AI systems compare to the human brain.

## What I Learned
In this course, I learned how artificial intelligence relates to neuroscience concepts such as memory, attention, perception, and cognitive processing. Through experiments, I was able to compare how AI systems function differently from the human brain. This helped me understand both the strengths and limitations of AI.
